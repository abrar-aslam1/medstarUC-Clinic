What we treat

Primary Care at Medstar covers the full range — here’s what we see most often, with what to expect from your visit.

Annual physicals & wellness

Comprehensive head-to-toe, age-appropriate screenings, prevention plan.

Diabetes management

A1c monitoring, medication adjustment, nutrition counseling, CGM support.

Hypertension & cholesterol

BP tracking, medication titration, lifestyle coaching.

Mental health

Depression & anxiety screening, medication management, referrals to therapy.

Thyroid & hormone

Lab workup, treatment, monitoring.

Geriatric care

Medicare Annual Wellness, fall prevention, cognitive screening.

What this isn’t for

We’re a family clinic, not a specialty hospital. If you need:

  • Same-day care, walk into urgent care — same physicians, no appointment needed
  • Specialty care (cardiology, oncology) — we’ll refer you and stay involved
  • Surgery or procedures we don’t perform on-site

Pricing & insurance

Most insurances cover annual visits at 100%. Self-pay primary visits start at $159. Lab work priced separately.
