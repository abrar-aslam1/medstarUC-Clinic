FAQs

When should I schedule my child’s first pediatric visit?

We recommend scheduling your baby’s first checkup within 3–5 days after birth. This crucial first visit allows our pediatricians to assess your newborn’s health, discuss feeding patterns, and address any immediate concerns.

What does a well-child visit include?

Our comprehensive well-child visits encompass physical examinations, growth monitoring, developmental screenings, and required immunizations. We also provide nutrition guidance, behavioral assessments, and dedicated time to address your parenting questions and concerns.

What is your approach to behavioral and developmental concerns?

Our pediatricians utilize evidence-based screening tools to assess behavioral and developmental progress during regular checkups. If concerns arise, we provide comprehensive evaluations, create individualized treatment plans, and coordinate with specialists when necessary.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans, Medicare, and offer flexible payment options. Contact our office to verify your specific coverage.

Do you provide same-day sick appointments?

Yes, we reserve dedicated appointment slots each day for acute illness visits. Our goal is to provide prompt medical attention when your child is sick, typically offering same-day appointments for urgent concerns during regular office hours.
