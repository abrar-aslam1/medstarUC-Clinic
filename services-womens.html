FAQs

How often should I schedule a wellness check-up?

We recommend annual wellness check-ups for most women. However, the frequency may vary based on your age, medical history, and specific health conditions. These regular visits help detect potential health issues early and maintain optimal wellness.

When should I start getting mammograms?

Generally, women should begin regular mammogram screenings at age 40. However, if you have a family history of breast cancer or other risk factors, earlier screening may be recommended. Discuss your personal risk factors with our healthcare providers to determine the best screening schedule.

What are the common signs of PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)?

Common PCOS symptoms include irregular periods, acne, weight gain, excessive hair growth, and fertility challenges. Early diagnosis and management can help control symptoms and prevent complications. Our specialists provide comprehensive PCOS care and personalized treatment plans.

How can I manage severe PMS symptoms?

PMS management typically involves a combination of lifestyle changes, dietary modifications, and possibly medication. Our healthcare team can help develop a personalized plan that may include stress reduction techniques, exercise recommendations, and appropriate medical interventions.

What preventive screenings should I prioritize as I age?

Key preventive screenings include annual physical examinations, regular blood pressure checks, cholesterol screening, bone density tests (especially post-menopause), mammograms, and pap smears.
