Meet the team

Doctors who'll
know your name.

Two board-certified family physicians and two physician assistants — between them, fluent in English, Urdu, Hindi, Punjabi, and Arabic. The same providers see you for urgent care, primary care, and women's health.

Syeda Qadri, M.D.

Board-Certified Family Physician

Dr. Qadri was born in New York and earned her medical degree from Dow Medical College on scholarship. She completed her family-practice residency at Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center and is board-certified in family medicine.

In practice since 2011, she focuses on urgent care and women's health and is known for the compassion she brings to every visit. She is fluent in Urdu and Hindi, practices alongside her husband, and spends her free time reading.

Family medicine Women's health Urdu · Hindi Since 2011

Hassan Imam, M.D.

Board-Certified Family Physician

Dr. Imam completed his training at Texas Tech University in Amarillo in 2006 and is board-certified in family medicine. He practices across our urgent and primary care clinics in Murphy and Richardson, offering a wide range of treatment options.

He has served as a hospitalist, primary-care physician, and urgent-care provider, and has had the privilege of working at Texas Tech University as an attending physician. Outside of clinic, he enjoys time with family, swimming, and painting. He speaks Hindi, Punjabi, and Urdu.

Family medicine Urgent care Hindi · Punjabi · Urdu Since 2006

Sarah Zaidi

Physician Assistant · PA-C

Sarah is a board-certified Physician Assistant with a strong educational and professional background in healthcare. She completed her PA training at West Coast University–Richardson, where she built the clinical knowledge and hands-on skills required to deliver high-quality patient care. She earned her undergraduate degree from the University of Texas at Dallas.

Outside of clinic, Sarah maintains an active, well-rounded life — reading, watching sports, traveling, and spending time with friends and family. That balance shows up in her practice as a compassionate, approachable provider committed to both personal and professional growth.

PA-C Primary care UT Dallas

Subrien Almahrooq

Physician Assistant · PA-C

Subrien is a board-certified Physician Assistant licensed to practice in the state of Texas within the scope of medicine. She completed her training at the University of Texas Medical Branch and her undergraduate degree at the University of North Texas.

In her free time, she likes to travel, explore new places, and play sports. She is fluent in Arabic.

PA-C Urgent care Arabic
