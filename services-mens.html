Comprehensive men’s health solutions

  • Low testosterone management
  • Prostate health & BPH

  • Testicular pain & swelling
  • UTI treatment

FAQs

Do I need a referral to see a men’s health specialist?

No referral is needed. Our care model allows direct access to our specialists. We accept most major insurance plans and offer same-day appointments for urgent concerns.

How private are men’s health consultations?

We maintain strict confidentiality for all patient visits. Our private consultation rooms and discrete check-in process ensure your comfort and privacy throughout your care journey.

Can I schedule both primary care and specialized treatments in one visit?

Yes. Our integrated care model means you can address multiple health concerns in a single visit. Our physicians are qualified to treat both routine and specialized men’s health issues.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans, Medicare, and offer flexible payment options. Contact our office to verify your specific coverage.

Do you provide ongoing care for chronic conditions?

Absolutely. Our physicians specialize in long-term management of conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and hormone imbalances, ensuring consistent, comprehensive care.
