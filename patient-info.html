FAQ

The questions we hear most.

Do I need an appointment?

For urgent care, no — walk in any time during open hours. For primary care, scheduling is faster but we accommodate same-day when we can.

What's the average wait?

About 11 minutes door-to-room across both locations. Real-time wait estimates are on our locations page.

Do you treat children?

Yes — pediatric care is one of our service lines. Same physicians for the whole family from newborn through adult.

Can I see results online?

Yes. Lab results post to the patient portal usually within 24 hours, often same-day for in-house tests.

Do you do telehealth?

For follow-ups, refills, and certain acute issues — yes. New patients are seen in person first.
