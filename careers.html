Why work here

The basics, done right.

We pay competitively, offer health benefits from day one, and protect your time off. The rest of what makes us different is harder to put in a benefits brochure — but it's the reason most of our team has stayed for years.

01 / TIME

Real visit lengths

15-minute slots are not a target here. New-patient primary visits are 40 minutes. Chronic-care follow-ups are 25. You'll have time to actually listen.

02 / TEAM

No corporate layer

No regional VP. No quarterly RVU pressure. Decisions about care, staffing, and scheduling get made in the building, by people who see patients themselves.

03 / GROWTH

Room to grow

CME budget, conference time, and mentorship from senior physicians. We support certifications, fellowships, and clinical interests outside of bread-and-butter primary care.

04 / BENEFITS

Health, dental, vision

Full medical, dental, and vision from day one. 401(k) with match. Malpractice fully covered for clinicians. Discounted care for immediate family.

05 / SCHEDULE

Predictable hours

Clinic hours are Monday–Friday with rotating Saturday mornings. No overnight call. PTO that actually gets taken — we cover for each other.

06 / CULTURE

A clinic, not a chain

We eat lunch together. We know each other's kids' names. New hires get a real onboarding, not a slide deck. The waiting-room plants are alive.
